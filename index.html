Tangible Code

Tangible code is an interactive installation design to teach the basics of web programming in a physical environment. The example is built with exerpts from Dr. Suess's short stories.

YERTLE THE TURTLE

I’m Yertle the Turtle!
Oh, marvelous me!
For I am the ruler
of all that I see!

You have brains in your head. You have feet in your shoes. You can steer yourself any direction you choose.

Your Majesty. Please…
I don’t like to complain.
But down here below,
we are feeling great pain.
